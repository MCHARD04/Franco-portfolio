About
"Two disciplines.
One standard."

I'm Franco Fradius, a 2nd-year Computer Science student at NIT. Most people pick a lane — code or design. I chose both deliberately, because I believe the best digital work requires fluency in both languages.

My CS coursework covers algorithms, data structures, networking, and systems. My design work spans brand identity, poster design, UI/UX, and visual communication. I use each discipline to make the other sharper.

I'm building toward a career at the intersection of engineering and creativity — shipping software that is both technically sound and unmistakably considered.

  • Full-stack execution, not just mockups
  • Design-first thinking on every build
  • Clean, documented, maintainable code
  • Reliable delivery on agreed timelines
